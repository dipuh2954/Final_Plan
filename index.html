Your 3 Daily Coding Slots
📱 6:00 AM · Commute Slot · Tue–Sat
LeetCode on Phone · 2–3 Problems Medium to Hard, no hints. Narrate your approach mentally while looking at the problem. Check email for OA invitations. Target: 250+ solved by W80.
💻 10:00 PM · Night Block · Tue–Sat
MacBook · 1 Hour · Main Coding Session Android features, architecture patterns, project building. After W81: OA debriefs, pipeline tracking, follow-up emails. 11 PM strict sleep — never compromise.
🖥️ 3:30 PM · Android Block · Sat/Sun/Mon
MacBook · 2 Hours · Heavy Tasks New features, architecture migrations, project builds needing focus. Plus 9:30 AM study block (3h) and 7 PM study block (2.5h) on weekends for theory and DSA review.
